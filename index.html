Project Overview

This research project, conducted under the RIML Research Group at Sharif University of Technology, focuses on applying Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL) to predictive maintenance in industrial systems. By using advanced reinforcement learning techniques, the project aims to optimize maintenance schedules, reduce downtime, and improve operational efficiency. The collaboration includes experts in machine learning and industrial engineering.
